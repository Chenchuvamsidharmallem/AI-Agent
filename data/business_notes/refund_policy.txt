Refund Policy

Customers can request a refund within 30 calendar days of purchase for standard plans.
Enterprise customers may request a refund review within 45 days when onboarding blockers are documented.
Refund requests related to missing analytics features should be routed to customer success before approval.
If the issue is caused by implementation confusion, the support team should offer a guided setup session first.
Full refunds are usually approved when the product cannot meet a documented customer requirement.
