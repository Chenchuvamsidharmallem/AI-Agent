Product Notes

Insight Pro is the premium analytics offering and is usually sold to enterprise or mid-market customers.
Insight Lite is the lower-cost entry product and often attracts SMB buyers who may later upgrade.
Forecast AI has strong demand among finance teams because it improves planning visibility.
Dashboard Plus customers sometimes need extra onboarding support for integrations and reporting setup.
Sales leadership encourages bundling training sessions with Dashboard Plus deals when the customer is new to BI tooling.
